Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

We aren't seeing much of a correlation here between these two variables, though one could assert that OpenWeather commonly characterizes cloud cover through intervals of 20, which probably are a way to quantify terms like "mostly cloudy" or "slightly cloudy".

There is a pretty constant variation within this dataset between 0% and 100% cloudiness.
